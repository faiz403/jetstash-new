What JetStash checks

Four things, every time

Is it actually direct? A route only shows Direct once a current, named source confirms it, for that specific airline, not just the route.

When's the fare likely to move? Booking-window guidance tied to a real festival or fare record, never a countdown timer.

Will your documents be ready? Passport validity and visa timing checked against your actual travel dates.

Has a fare actually been checked? If nobody's logged a complete, dated fare check yet, we say so, never a stale or invented price.
